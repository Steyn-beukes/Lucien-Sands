Are You Looking For The Perfect Getaway?

Lucien Sands 501 is the right place for you!


Here at Luicien Sands, we strive to give you the Best holiday possible.

Lucien Sands 501 is a self-catering apartment located in a secure complex, situated in Margate. The apartment offers lovely 180-degree ocean views from the balcony and most rooms.

This 4th-floor apartment can accommodate 6 persons, and consists of 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The main bedroom is furnished with a queen-size bed, and features an en-suite bathroom with a corner bath and a shower. The second bedroom is equipped with a double bed, and opens onto a small balcony that offers sea views, and the third bedroom has 2 single beds. These 2 rooms share the second bathroom, which has a shower and a bath.

The well-equipped kitchen contains a fridge, a microwave, an oven with a stove, a dishwasher, and a washing machine. The open-plan lounge features a smart TV and speedy WI-FI. The lounge leads to a spacious balcony, which boasts a gas braai facility.

The complex also boasts an elevator, a swimming pool, and secure allocated parking. In addition, the complex is ideally situated within 3 km from Margate Main Beach, Hibiscus Mall Centre, restaurants, and shops to visit.
